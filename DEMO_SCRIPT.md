# Aiva Demonstration Script: The Closed-Loop Employee Experience

**Theme:** "From First Day to Daily Engagement in Minutes"
**Duration:** 5-7 minutes

---

### Introduction: The Employee Lifecycle Challenge (30 seconds)

**(Start at the main welcome screen)**

**Presenter:** "Good morning. For many companies, managing the entire employee lifecycle—from onboarding hundreds of seasonal workers to keeping permanent staff engaged—is a massive logistical challenge. It's slow, error-prone, and creates compliance risks. Today, we'll show you how Aiva solves this entire problem in under seven minutes, creating a seamless and compliant experience from start to finish."

---

### Scene 1: The Manager's Preparation (1 minute)

**Presenter:** "Our story begins not with the worker, but with the manager, before the first day even starts."

*   **Action:** Select the **"Employee / Manager"** role from the welcome screen.
*   **Presenter:** "This is the employee dashboard. As a manager, I have access to advanced tools. Let's go to my command center."
*   **Action:** Click **"Manager Dashboard"** in the sidebar.
*   **Presenter:** "To prepare for our new hire, Kobus Dlamini, we'll go to the **QR Code Generator**."
*   **Action:** Click the "QR Code Generator" tab.
*   **Presenter:** "I can quickly find any worker in the system."
*   **Action:** In the search bar, type "Kobus Dlamini". His record appears.
*   **Presenter:** "**Here is a key highlight:** Aiva already knows Kobus's preferred language. This QR code is personalized, so his entire onboarding experience will be in the language he's most comfortable with, ensuring perfect clarity."
*   **Action:** Click the **"Generate QR"** button. The QR code appears in a modal.
*   **Presenter:** "This unique, secure code is now ready. The manager can email it, print it, or send it via message. Kobus now has his key to a seamless first day. Let's switch to his perspective."

---

### Scene 2: The Worker's First Day (1 minute)

*   **Action:** Close the QR modal and click the Aiva logo in the top-left to trigger the **"Reset Kiosk Session?"** confirmation, then confirm. This returns you to the main welcome screen.
*   **Presenter:** "Kobus arrives at the kiosk. No complex menus, no confusion. He simply identifies himself as a **Seasonal Worker**."
*   **Action:** Click the **"Seasonal Worker"** button. The onboarding flow begins.
*   **Presenter:** "No typing, no searching for his name. He just scans the code his manager sent."
*   **Action:** Click **"Scan QR Code"** and scan the code for Kobus Dlamini.
*   **Presenter (WOW Moment):** "And instantly, the kiosk greets him by name—'Welcome, Kobus!'—and guides him to the first step: document submission."

---

### Scene 3: Intelligent Document Processing (1.5 minutes)

**(The screen now shows the worker's document checklist)**

*   **Presenter:** "Aiva provides a clear checklist. Let's start with his ID document."
*   **Action:** Click **"Capture"** next to "ID Document". The camera view opens.
*   **Presenter:** "He holds his ID up, and Aiva's intelligent frame guides him for a perfect shot."
*   **Action:** Capture the image. The "Analyzing..." screen appears.
*   **Presenter:** "This is where the magic happens. The image is securely processed by Google's Gemini AI. This isn't just OCR; it *understands* the document, extracting the name, ID number, and date of birth into a structured format."
*   **(The verification screen appears with the extracted data.)**
*   **Presenter:** "The data is presented back for one-click confirmation. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors by over 90%, and saves a huge amount of HR administration time."
*   **Action:** Click **"Confirm & Continue"**. The checklist updates to show "ID Document" as verified.

---

### Scene 4: Policy Understanding & Compliance (1 minute)

*   **Presenter:** "After documents, compliance is key. But compliance is more than just checking a box; it's about understanding. Aiva ensures employees are informed from day one."
*   **Action:** Click **"Continue to Next Step"** on the checklist, then capture a profile photo to complete the onboarding flow. After the "Onboarding Complete!" screen, reset the kiosk.
*   **Action:** Select **"Employee / Manager"** again to enter the main portal. In the sidebar, click **"HR Policies"**.
*   **Presenter:** "Here in the HR Policies portal, an employee can explore the entire company manual. Let's say they have a question about leave."
*   **Action:** Click **"Leave Policies"** in the sidebar. The summary appears.
*   **Presenter:** "Instead of reading the whole document, they can just ask Aiva."
*   **Action:** Click the **"Analyze with Aiva"** button. The Co-Pilot will open and instantly provide a summary.
*   **Presenter:** "This demonstrates Aiva's ability to act as a true co-pilot, using its knowledge base to give employees instant, accurate answers."

---

### Scene 5: Closing the Loop (30 seconds)

*   **Action:** Navigate back to the **Manager Dashboard** and the **Seasonal Workforce** tab.
*   **Presenter:** "Finally, to close the loop, let's return to the manager's view. With no delay, the dashboard is updated in real-time."
*   **Action:** Point to the dashboard stats and the worker list where Kobus is now "Completed".
*   **Presenter (WOW Moment):** "The onboarding progress ticks up, the compliance rate is updated, and we can see that Kobus Dlamini's status is now 'Completed'. The manager has full visibility from anywhere, at any time."

---

### Scene 6: Frictionless Daily Tasks (1 minute)

**Presenter:** "Aiva also streamlines daily tasks for all employees. Let's see how simple checking in for the day is."

*   **Action:** From the employee dashboard, open the **Time & Attendance Kiosk** from the sidebar.
*   **Presenter:** "Instead of searching for their name, an employee can use the most secure form of identification they have. This is a fantastic way to see Gemini's multimodal power in a low-risk, high-impact scenario."
*   **Action:** Click the **"Scan ID / Passport"** button. The camera view opens.
*   **Presenter:** "Just like with onboarding, Aiva uses the camera to scan the document. You can use any valid ID or even a driver's license for this."
*   **Action:** Capture an image of an ID card (a sample can be used).
*   **(The screen shows a 'Verifying...' message, then transitions to the action screen for that employee.)**
*   **Presenter (WOW Moment):** "And just like that, Aiva reads the name from the ID, finds the employee's profile, and presents them with their clock-in options. It's fast, secure, and completely frictionless."
*   **Action:** Click **"Check In"**. The confirmation flow continues.

---

### Conclusion (30 seconds)

**Presenter:** "So, in just a few minutes, we've seen the complete, closed-loop experience. We took a seasonal worker from manager preparation to fully compliant and ready-to-work. And we've seen how Aiva transforms into a daily productivity hub for permanent staff. We've eliminated paperwork, overcome language barriers, and created a powerful, continuous engagement and audit trail. That is the efficiency, compliance, and experience Aiva delivers."
*   **(Optional: Click "Aiva Vision" in the sidebar to show the blueprint slides as you give your final thoughts.)**
*   **Presenter:** "Thank you."
