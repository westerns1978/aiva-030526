## 1. Executive Summary
Aiva 2.0 is an **Agentic Operational Middleware** designed to transition manual franchise workflows into a digital data mesh. It automates the high-friction "Numerical Induction Sequence" (Onboarding), manages clinical office compliance, and provides real-time Business Intelligence (BI) through an integrated neural interface.

## 3. The Numerical Induction Sequence (1-6)
A deterministic state machine for onboarding new hires:

1.  **Offer to Employ:** Verification of digital signature via WhatsApp secure tunnel.
2.  **Identity:** High-speed capture and filing of South African ID Books or Passports.
3.  **Residence:** Proof of Address verification (Utility bills/Bank letters).
4.  **Remuneration:** Ingestion of Bank Confirmation letters for salary routing.
5.  **Benefits:** Enrollment in Medical Aid and Provident Fund nodes.
6.  **Digital Ecosystem:** Final calibration and provisioning of Nashua Email and iNForm CRM credentials.

## 4. Systems Architecture

### 4.1 Data Fabric (Supabase)
*   **PostgreSQL:** Persists telemetry, employee records, and dynamic agent knowledge.

### 4.2 Universal Client (`westflowClient.ts`)
